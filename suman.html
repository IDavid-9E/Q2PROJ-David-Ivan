Suman

Ingredients

  • • 2 cups glutinous rice
  • • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • • 1/4 tsp salt
  • • Banana leaves, softened

Instructions

  1. Wash the glutinous rice thoroughly and soak in water for at least 2 hours. Drain well.
  2. In a pot, combine coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Cook over low heat until the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Add the soaked glutinous rice to the pot and cook until the liquid is absorbed, stirring occasionally. Let it cool slightly.
  4. Prepare the banana leaves by passing them over low heat to make them pliable.
  5. Scoop about 2-3 tablespoons of the rice mixture and place it on the banana leaf. Roll tightly and fold the edges to seal.
  6. Steam the suman rolls for about 30-40 minutes or until fully cooked.
  7. Serve warm or at room temperature, optionally with latik sauce or sugar on the side.
