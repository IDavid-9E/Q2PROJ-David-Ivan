Halo-Halo

Ingredients

  • • 1/2 cup shaved ice
  • • 1/2 cup sweetened saba bananas
  • • 1/4 cup sweetened jackfruit (langka)
  • • 1/4 cup red mung beans (sweetened)
  • • 1/4 cup sweetened macapuno (coconut sport)
  • • 1/4 cup nata de coco
  • • 1/4 cup sweetened purple yam (ube halaya)
  • • 2 tbsp leche flan (caramel custard)
  • • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • • 1 scoop ube or vanilla ice cream

Instructions

  1. Prepare a tall glass or bowl for serving.
  2. Add layers of sweetened saba bananas, jackfruit, mung beans, macapuno, nata de coco, and purple yam at the bottom.
  3. Top with shaved ice, ensuring it fills the glass or bowl.
  4. Drizzle evaporated milk over the shaved ice.
  5. Add a scoop of ice cream on top.
  6. Garnish with leche flan and more purple yam, if desired.
  7. Serve immediately with a spoon and enjoy!
