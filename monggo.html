Monggo (Mung Bean Soup)

Ingredients

  • • 1 cup mung beans, rinsed
  • • 5 cups water
  • • 200 grams pork belly, sliced into small pieces
  • • 2 cups spinach or malunggay leaves
  • • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • • 1/2 cup chicharon (pork cracklings), optional
  • • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, boil the mung beans in water until tender (about 30-40 minutes). Set aside.
  2. In a separate pan, sauté garlic, onion, and tomatoes until softened.
  3. Add the pork belly and cook until browned.
  4. Pour the boiled mung beans (along with the liquid) into the pan. Mix well.
  5. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the spinach or malunggay leaves and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. If desired, sprinkle chicharon on top before serving.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.
