Sinigang na Baboy

Ingredients

  • • 1 kg pork belly, cut into chunks
  • • 1 liter water
  • • 2 medium-sized tomatoes, quartered
  • • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • • 1 radish, sliced
  • • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach)
  • • 5-6 okra, halved
  • • 1 cup tamarind mix (or fresh tamarind)
  • • Salt and fish sauce to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, boil the pork belly in water until tender.
  2. Add the tomatoes and onion. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix in the radish and okra. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the tamarind mix or fresh tamarind. Stir well.
  5. Season with salt and fish sauce to taste.
  6. Add the kangkong and let it wilt. Turn off the heat.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
