Ginataang Hipon

Ingredients

  • • 1 kg fresh shrimp, cleaned
  • • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • • 2 cups squash, cubed
  • • 1 bunch string beans, cut into 2-inch lengths
  • • 2 pieces green chili (siling haba)
  • • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pan, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the squash and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Add the string beans and shrimp. Cook until shrimp turns pink.
  5. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the green chili for extra flavor. Simmer for another 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
